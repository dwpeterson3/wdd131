Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide meaningful information to customers of 208 Food Hub. It will provide information about the business and what products and services it can provide to its customers.

Audience

The intended audience is going to be anyone in the southeast Idaho area.

Dynamic elements

I am planning to have a menu child page that will have images of some of the menu items. These images will be responsive and will use modals and pop up like our cool pics assignment. I will also utilize an interactive menu button that will show the nav links when utilizing a mobile device. I also want to have the menu items be objects like the blog assignment and show different menu items instead of books.
